What is AI?

Artifical intelligence is the use of technology to give computers intelligence. AI is everywhere in the world around us, from self-driving cars, to YouTube, to voice assistants. Often AI is misunderstood as some magical tool only geniuses in Silicon Valley can play with, but this is not the case. By the time you finish using this app, you will be enlightened with how this state of the art technology works. Machine learning, artifical intelligence, and neural networks are often used interchangeably, but there is a difference which I demonstrate below.

Artifical Intelligence is any technology that replicates human intelligence in any way.

Machine learning achieves artificial intelligence through data.
Neural networks are machine learning models that use the architecture of the human brain to achieve artifical intelligence through a network of neurons.
